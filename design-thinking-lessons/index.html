Mission Resources Notes / ground rules

How this works

Every lesson pairs a short, real Next.js/TypeScript (or, in the broadening pass, PHP/MySQL) case study with a build-it-yourself exercise. Try the exercise first, then expand the model answer underneath it — comparing your own reasoning against a concrete verdict, not just reading the lesson, is what actually makes the vocabulary stick. Each lesson also links a companion cheat sheet for quick reference later.
