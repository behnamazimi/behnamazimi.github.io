Incident Response Cheat Sheet

Terms

Severity level
Classification (often Sev1–Sev4) of how serious an incident is and how urgently it needs response — tracks impact, not diff size.
Blast radius
How many users, requests, or how much data were actually affected.
Blameless postmortem
A write-up assuming good intent, focused on contributing causes and mechanisms, not individual fault.

Order of operations

  1. Triage — confirm it's real, assess severity and blast radius.
  2. Mitigate — stop the bleeding with whatever blunt tool works (even at a cost, like disabling a cache).
  3. Resolve — the real, reviewed fix.
  4. Postmortem — what happened, impact, root cause, action items that don't depend on someone "being more careful."
